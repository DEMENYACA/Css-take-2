Meatballs

  • 1lb. Ground Beef
  • 1lb.Ground Pork Sausage
  • 2 Cloves Garlic Grated
  • 1 White Onion grated
  • 1/2 cup fresh Parmesan grated
  • 1/4 cup Romano grated
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped Italian Parsley
  • 1/4 cup freshe chopped Oregano
  • 2 slices fresh baked bread torn
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 whole eggs beatened
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/4 tsp cracked black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp Red peper flakes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • small bowl combine beatened eggs, cream and torn bread pieces set aside.
  • A large bowl combine Beef, Sausage and all the other ingredients
  • Add egg mixture mix again until well combined
  • Large sheet pan fitted with wire rack.
  • Bowl of ice water set aside
  • Wet hands with ice water, grab meat mixture shape into desired meatball sizes
  • Arrange atop wire rack
  • Bake at 400 for 20-25min
  • Meatballs can be then placed into tomato sauce to finish cooking
